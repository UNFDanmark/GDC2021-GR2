Bouncability Battle Arena!


Det klassiske Y8 spil AZ-tanks er nu blevet genskabt i en ekstra dimension!
BBA er en ny rendering af AZ-tanks, men nu i 3D.


Controls:
W,A,S,D - Player 1 Movement
Spacebar - Player 1 Shoot
R - Player 1 reset tank orientation
^,<,v,> - Player 2 Movement
P - Player 2 Shoot
L - Player 2 reset tank orientation
